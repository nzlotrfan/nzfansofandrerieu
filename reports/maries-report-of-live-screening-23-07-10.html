Hi Reuben and all.
My friend Yvonne and I thoroughly enjoyed the viewing of the Maastricht concert at the Reading Cinema in Wellington recently. We especially enjoyed the "Black Diamond" and her children's choir. What a wonderful way to view a fantastic concert, it was almost like being there and would have been more so, if the audience had of participated by swaying and clapping like being there. This was well worth the money and has made us really excited, as we are traveling to the Melbourne concert later this year. For those who can't afford to, or don't get the opportunity to attend a concert, this is certainly enjoyable and a viable alternative.
Marie Mackley
