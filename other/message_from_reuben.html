Hey folks!!

Wow! What an exciting Monday it was today!! Thanks soooo much for all your txt votes today! It was totally worth it, because,,, I GOT IN! Yay!!!! – To the grand finale! And it wouldn’t have happened without you all! I just want to say a BIG THANK YOU for all the people who voted for me today, and for all the people who have always supported me. It’s been an entertaining, stressing, but worth it past couple of days. All of my grandparents learnt how to txt in the weekend, preparing for today, and my Mum, Dad, Sister, my neighbours from across the road, and the rest of my family and friends all put in such an awesome effort to encourage people to watch and vote for me. So thanks to everyone once again.

Now my thank yous are over, what happened?? I was in Hamilton for the regional barbershop competition, I was a part of our schools barber quartet. The day had come to an end, and we had stopped at the local McDonalds in Hamilton. I was starting to think that maybe I didn’t make it through as it was just about 4pm. Anyway, at 3:44pm, I got the call from a TVNZ Good Morning person congratulating me and saying I made it to the grand finale!! It was very exciting, I spent a long time on the phone with her discussing things about me, and technical things for getting down to Wellington. What a crazy afternoon?!?

So what happens now?? I will be traveling to Wellington this Thursday afternoon and staying the night down there, ready to appear on the Good Morning show the following morning in Avalon Studios. I’ll be performing ‘Call Me Irresponsible’ in front of a live studio audience plus three judges and you guys at home battling it out against four other contestants. The judges are Robert Rakete, Jackie Clarke and Stacey Morrison.

Yes, I still need your help!! There will be another voting system on the day, but because of the judges, it will be similar to Dancing With The Stars. The three judges will give a number score on each performance, holding up a sign saying ‘9’, ‘6’, ‘7’ for example. And then the TV audience will once again txt vote for their favourite performer. The txt votes will be added together along with the judges’ scores to determine the overall winner which is announced live on the show.
