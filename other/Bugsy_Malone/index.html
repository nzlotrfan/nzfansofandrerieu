Wow! What a week huh?! We all did such an AWESOME job! The show was a success! The sound, lighting, filming, acting, music, stage set, props, prompters all ran perfectly last night. We can all now sit back and relax and be proud of what we've all together accomplished.

I've managed to put a few photos up on my Facebook (no FB account needed!). These are just my photos though, be sure to check other peoples' Facebook/Bebo profiles out later on as other people will upload their photos when they can too.

Well done everyone for the hard work put in. It paid off didn't it? Well done.
