NCEA Logbook Excel Spreadsheet

Some Microsoft Office Excel knowledge is required; as you may have to format it to suit the amount of subjects/assessments you have.

Click on the picture below to see a screenshot of the Logbook.

A few things to note...

  • The screenshot above is from a 1920 by 1080 pixel monitor. There will be scrolling required on monitors smaller than this.
  • Deleting columns C - P to suit the amount of assessments you have will not affect the formulas.
  • Deleting a whole subject will not affect the formulas also.
  • NSN = National Student Number
  • Works with Microsoft Office Excel 2003, 2007 (not sure about older versions).

I did not create this spreadsheet, I downloaded it for free from Microsoft Templates. However, I did change the colour scheme, jargon (previously written for the American schooling system) and made it user friendly for Microsoft 2003 users.
