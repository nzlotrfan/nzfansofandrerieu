Hey there folks!

The Good Morning TV show that airs weekdays at 9:00am - 12:00pm on TV1, have recently held a talent competition. The deal was that the entrant had to create a short 90 second DVD extract showcasing their talent. If the entrant is selected, the entrant goes into the final 15, then New Zealand audience must TXT vote to put through their favourite performer into the final 5, then the winner is selected by a panel of judges.

The winner receives a pretty alright package!!! - A trip to Australia!!!!, "...Not only that but you'll also recieve a career design package from agent and country singer Gray Bartlett, who has launched the careers of stars like Hayley Westenra and Yulia. And, if you are a singer or instrumentalist, a recording of a song." and a few more awesome things!

SOOOOoooo!!
Last weekend (30th & 31st May), we (Mum, Dad & my sister Anneliese) recorded a video of me singing 'Call Me Irresponsible', and on Tuesday sent it down to Good Morning HQ at Avalon Studios, in Wellington. What an exciting weekend it was!

Anyway! Today (Friday 5th June) the competition closed at 11:00am. I got a phone call on my cellphone at school during my last class, (luckily I was outside when the phone rang!) to be told I made the final 15!!! WOW!!

So now I need your help!!!
My video will be aired on Good Morning this Monday at 10:30AM exactly (show starts at 9:00AM though), so what I need you to do.... I am contestant 'E'. After my video has played, you can vote for me by txting "E" (without quotations) to 8687. But.... I know lots of you work or won't be available to watch it, so therefore, the voting lines will be open from 10:35am Monday and will close when the show finishes, so you would need to vote before 11:45am to be safe.
