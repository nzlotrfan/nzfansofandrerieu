The Homecoming (2006) - 167 Minutes
 
This new multimedia spectacular was filmed live in Maastricht, The Netherlands.

Filmed in the Netherlands, Andre Rieu's THE HOMECOMING features over 30 classical tracks including "Lullaby," "Carmen Overture," "Auld Lang Syne," and "Swinging Bells."
