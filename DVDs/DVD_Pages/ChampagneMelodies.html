Champagne Melodies (2009)
 
Champagne, Strauss and celebration! André Rieu brings you a stirring concert of music to make your heart beat in three-quarter time and make your emotions fizz and bubble like champagne. The world's favourite waltzes, marches and melodies on a new concert on DVD held on a cold and crisp New Year's Eve in Germany.
