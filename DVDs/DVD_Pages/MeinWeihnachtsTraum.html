Mein Wihnachts Traum (1997) - 75 Minutes
 
This Christmas special with its magical images is certain to get you into the Christmas spirit. Relax to the sounds of 'Silent Night' and other well-known Christmas melodies and enjoy dreaming with André, who shows on this video how he celebrates Christmas: with music!
