Live In Sydney (2009)
 
Filmed in October 2009 during André Rieu’s triumphant Australian return, this DVD features all of Andre’s famous trademarks – glorious music, beautiful costumes, enthralled audiences and plenty of wonderful surprise guests including Australia’s best-known radio personality Alan Jones and the first lady herself, Dame Edna Everage.
