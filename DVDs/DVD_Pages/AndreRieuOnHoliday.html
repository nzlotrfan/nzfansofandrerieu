Andre Rieu On Holiday (2007) - 165 Minutes
 
Go on holiday with Andre Rieu with this very special 2-DVD set of two concerts never previously released on DVD in Australia, bringing you 50 amazing songs and taking you right through the Christmas holidays to bring in the New Year – this year, every year…

This Christmas Spectacular with its magical images is certain to get you into the Christmas spirit. Relax to the sounds of Silent Night and O Tannenbaum, dream of a White Christmas as Andre Rieu shares this very special Christmas with you!

What would you say to an evening of partying with music, song and above all, lots of fun? This DVD won't just have you in party mood on New Year's Eve; it could be party night every night! This scintillating New Year's Eve concert was recorded in December 2003 in Hanover, Germany, with André Rieu and his orchestra, and accompanied by special guests Bond and Russell Watson, showing what partying should be all about. It's truly Andre at his liveliest best!
