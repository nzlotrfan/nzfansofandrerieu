Romantic Moments (2009) - 45 Minutes
 
For these pictures we travelled to Majorca, where it's always sunny, and we found the perfect setting, near the mediaeval Villa Raixa, for footage to go with the music on the CD 'Romantic Moments'. Well, perhaps 'always sunny' was a bit optimistic! On one occasion it rained so hard and for so long that André fell asleep, sitting on a chair outside waiting for the sun to come out, with an umbrella in one hand and his violin in the other...
