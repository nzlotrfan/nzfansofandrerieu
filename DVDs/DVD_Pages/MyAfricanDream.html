My African Dream (2010)
 
The Dutch Soccer Team had a dream in South Africa; so did André Rieu. Team Orange did not quite get to realize their dream, but André Rieu did. After a few promotional visits to the country, he performed a number of concerts accompanied by his entire Johann Strauss Orchestra. The results were astonishing and the enthusiasm of the concert audiences was unprecedented and was at times accompanied by many emotions. The African singer Kimmy Skota and the children of the Bloemfontein choir and the Hout Bay Music project were true local stars who made a great impression on the audience.

'My African Dream' is a double DVD of André's adventures in South Africa, complemented by his complete Johann Strauss Orchestra.

Join André on his African adventure in this magnificent 2 Disc Set! Watch the concert in it's full glory, and go behind the scenes in a fascinating 5-part documentary.
