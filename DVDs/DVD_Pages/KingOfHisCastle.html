André Rieu - King Of His Castle (Limited Edition) (2009)
 
Among André Rieu's many claims to fame is the fact that he lives in a castle, famous for, among other things the kitchen where d'Artagnan had his last meal! It's been the subject of many an interview and features prominently on his DVD 'My Music, My Life', and now this ultimate gift package, with its design based on André's own castle, is released to celebrate André's first ever New Zealand tour. It features a collection of best-selling DVDs -- nine single and one 2-DVD set (eleven discs in total).

The collection includes his best-selling Schönbrunn, Wonderland, Maastricht and New York concerts, Christmas and New Year concerts, beautifully-filmed clips ('Dreaming') and a documentary ('A Fairy Tale').
