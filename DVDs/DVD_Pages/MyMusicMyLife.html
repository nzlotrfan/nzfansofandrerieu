My Music, My Life (2009) - 79 Minutes
 
This new revealing documentary takes master musician, violinist & entertainer extraordinaire André Rieu's achievements to a new level as he brings Vienna to the world.

Relive his chart history in Holland and take a tour around André's castle home in Maastricht. Marvel as a replica of Schönbrunn Castle is transported around the globe and built in 40 seconds! Meet his fans in his new land of adoption, Australia.

Dutch TV personality Ivo Niehe invites you to share with him the world of the incredible man & musician that is André Rieu.
