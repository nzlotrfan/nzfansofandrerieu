I lost my heart in Heidelberg (2009) - 90 Minutes
 
That’s how you’ll feel too once you have seen this exciting new DVD – you will lose your heart to this beautiful, romantic city! Enjoy André and the Johann Strauss Orchestra, Mirusia Louwerse, Carla Maffioletti, the Platinum Tenors and the Berlin Comedian Harmonists. Attend a fabulous open-air concert with a romantic ruined castle as a setting. Let André take you along on his musical tour of the town, a boat trip with the orchestra on the river Neckar, and many more surprises...
