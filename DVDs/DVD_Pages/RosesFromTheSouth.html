Roses From The South (2010)
 
Themed around Johan Strauss' famous waltz of the same name, 'Roses From the South' sees André venturing to the amazing sub-tropical paradise island of Mainau, just off the south shore of Germany.

Known as the 'Flower Island' due it its unique exotic gardens, Mainau and its majestic palace grounds provide the perfect picturesque setting for André and his Johan Strauss Orchestra to perform a spellbinding set in front of a sea of adoring fans.
