Royal Albert Hall (2002) - 105 Minutes
 
This DVD has fantastic pictures of a very special concert at the Royal Albert Hall. Allow yourself to be carried away once more by the well-known melodies of `Tea for Two` and `Auld Lang Syne` and many other familiar classics. Enjoy André and his orchestra, the enthusiasm of the British audience and the fabulous concert hall. A lovely way to experience an unforgettable evening once more.
